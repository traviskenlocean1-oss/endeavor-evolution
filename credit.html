Our Approach

We Attack Every Angle Simultaneously

Most credit repair companies do one thing. We do all of them at the same time — which is why our clients see results faster.

Dispute Errors

Up to 79% of credit reports contain errors. We identify and dispute inaccurate negative items directly with all three bureaus — Equifax, Experian, TransUnion.

Remove Late Payments

We send goodwill letters and leverage consumer protection laws to request removal of late payments, charge-offs, and collections that may be hurting your score.

Reduce Inquiries

Excessive hard inquiries can drag your score down significantly. We challenge unauthorized or outdated inquiries and coach you on how to avoid more.

Build Positive History

We don't just remove the bad — we help you add the good. Secured cards, credit builder accounts, and authorized user strategies to build a positive payment history.

Optimize Utilization

Credit utilization accounts for 30% of your score. We advise on the optimal balance-to-limit ratio and strategies to improve it quickly without new credit.

Ongoing Monitoring

We monitor your reports throughout the process, alert you to new issues immediately, and adjust the strategy based on bureau responses.
