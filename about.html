Meet The Founder

Built On Connection.
Driven By Purpose.

Witson Locean didn't start Endeavor Evolution Enterprises because he saw a market opportunity. He started it because he lived the problem firsthand — watching talented, driven entrepreneurs fail not because they lacked vision, but because they lacked access.

Access to capital. Access to the right people. Access to the knowledge that could have changed everything. Witson built this company to be that access — a bridge that connects individuals, entrepreneurs, and small business owners to the people, resources, and services they need to grow.

Over the past 5+ years, Witson has worked directly with more than 500 clients across South Florida — from startups trying to get their first line of credit to established small businesses seeking six-figure capital injections. His approach is always the same: personal, direct, and relentlessly focused on results.

"My mission has always been simple — to create pathways that allow people to move forward."

— Witson Locean, Chief Connection Officer

As the founder of Endeavor Evolution and its sister brand Visionary Web Studio, Witson brings a complete ecosystem of services under one roof — ensuring that every client who walks through the door has access to everything they need to build something lasting.
